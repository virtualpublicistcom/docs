Press releases used to be complicated — endless drafts, confusing formats, and hours spent polishing every line. With Virtual Publicist, you can create and publish your press release in just a few clicks — whether you want full control or prefer the AI to handle the hard work.

Managing Your Press Releases with Virtual Publicist

After logging into Virtual Publicist, keeping track of your press releases is simple:

  1. From the left menu, select “Press Releases.”
  2. You’ll find a list of everything you’ve created in one place .

Each press release includes handy options:

  • Remove — Clear out the ones you don’t need anymore.
  • Share — Send your release out quickly and easily.
  • Add to New Campaign — Drop it into a fresh campaign with a single click.

These tools make organizing and updating your PR work effortless.

Two Ways to Write: Manual Mode vs. AI Agent

Every creator, entrepreneur, and brand has their own style. Virtual Publicist meets you there with two modes designed to fit your workflow.

1) Manual Mode — For Hands-On Creators

If you like shaping every detail yourself, manual mode is your playground. You can:

  • Craft headlines and write your text from scratch
  • Generate sections of text with built-in tools
  • Add or remove paragraphs and images as you go
  • Rearrange content with ease
  • Reset the text if you want a fresh start
  • Schedule a release date so your news goes live at the perfect time
  • Save your work and refine until it feels complete
  • Upload a cover image to make your announcement stand out

Think of manual mode as your digital press room — it gives you the structure, but you hold the pen.

2) AI Agent — For Speed & Simplicity

When time is short or inspiration is running low, the AI agent steps in like your personal PR assistant. With a single prompt, it can:

  • Generate complete, SEO-friendly press release text in minutes
  • Suggest headlines that grab attention
  • Build targeted media outreach lists so you know exactly who to contact
  • Prepare materials for YouTube videos, turning your news into multimedia campaigns

With AI agent mode, you don’t just save time — you gain confidence knowing your press release is crafted with expert-level quality.
