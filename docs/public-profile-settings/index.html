Virtual Publicist Settings: A Quick Setup Guide

Virtual Publicist (VP) is your personal PR assistant — built to make promotion effortless!
Want to grow your fanbase and gain worldwide popularity? Virtual Publicist is here for you.

Start by Building Your Profile on Virtual Publicist

  1. Open the Virtual Publicist homepage and click Create Account.
  2. First time here? Hit Sign Up to get started.
  3. Already joined? Simply Log In to jump back into your workspace.

After successfully signing up, you’ll be asked to share a few basic details to personalize your profile:

  • Genre: Select the main category that best describes your music style.
  • Tags: Add words that capture your sound, mood, or influences — e.g., “indie pop,” “trap,” “Afro-soul.”
  • Country/City: Mention where you’re based or where your music scene thrives.

After these quick steps, you’ll arrive at your Dashboard — the control center for everything you do on VP: - Manage Press Releases - Run PR Campaigns - Edit Profile Settings - And much more — all in one place.

Exploring the Settings

When you open Settings, you’ll see these sections:

  • Account
  • Profile Settings
  • Social Channels
  • Password
  • Appearance

Account — Update Your Details

Use Account to adjust your name and email address so updates, release notifications, and campaign messages reach the right place.

  • Changed your stage name or email? Update the fields and click Save.
  • Fast, simple, and keeps everything running smoothly.

Artist Profile — How to Polish It

Your profile is where your creative identity comes alive.

Path: Sidebar → your Name/Email → Settings

From there, you can: - Link your Spotify (your profile image syncs automatically).
- Update your artist name, genre, location, and tags anytime.
- Write a short bio that reflects your story and sound.

Tip: Keep the bio short, direct, and memorable. Mention influences, your journey, and your signature sound.

Don’t forget to hit Save when you’re done!

Social Channels — Boost Visibility With Linked Socials

In Social Channels, connect your: - Instagram
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Any other platform that showcases your music

This helps curators, journalists, and fans explore your world in a click — smooth and effortless.

Password — Protect Your Account

Under Password, you can update your password anytime.

  • Change it occasionally, especially if reused elsewhere.
  • Choose something strong: letters + numbers + symbols.

Appearance — Match Your Mood

Pick your preferred look for comfortable sessions, day or night:

  • Light Mode — bright, clean view
  • Dark Mode — softer, night-friendly layout
  • System Mode — matches your device’s theme automatically

One click and your dashboard updates instantly.
