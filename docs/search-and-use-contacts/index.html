Master the Search Bar: Your Gateway to Fast PR Connections

Every great campaign starts with the right people — and that’s where the search bar on Virtual Publicist comes in. Forget wasting hours on old databases or random websites. With VP’s search, you can discover perfect contacts in seconds.

Here’s the fun part: this tool isn’t just quick — it’s smart, intuitive, and built for artists who want real results without the stress.

How the Search Bar Works

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. In the sidebar, click Search to open the sleek search bar.
  3. Type a name, role, genre, outlet, or keyword — matching contacts appear instantly.

Each contact shows key info at a glance:

  • Company name
  • Location
  • Position
  • About
  • Genres
  • Tags

Everything appears in one place — no extra tabs, no chaos, just results.

Add Contacts in One Click

  • Found someone interesting? Click the three dots to Add to List or Add to Campaign directly.
  • Short on time? Use checkboxes to select multiple contacts and add them all at once.
  • Changed your mind? Hit Deselect All and start fresh.

Think of the search bar as your backstage pass to PR connections — organize, connect, and move faster than ever.

Why Artists Love the Search Bar

  • Speed: Find ideal contacts instantly
  • Clarity: See key details in one view
  • Control: Add or remove with one click

Whether you’re targeting playlists, blogs, or press, the search bar helps you find the right people — fast and easy.

Pricing Plans

Setting up your profile on Virtual Publicist is free — no strings attached. When you’re ready for advanced tools and full campaign features, you can choose a plan that matches your goals.

The Advanced plan is priced at $99 per month. If you prefer annual billing, the rate is $89 per month (billed annually), which results in a yearly saving of $120. This option includes deeper insights, smarter outreach tools, and enhanced campaign capabilities suitable for growing artists.

The Pro plan is priced at $249 per month. With annual billing, the rate becomes $224 per month (billed annually), saving $300 over the year. This option unlocks every premium feature in Virtual Publicist, from AI-driven campaign tools to advanced analytics and robust contact management.

No matter where you are in your music journey, there’s a plan that fits your pace and scales with you.
