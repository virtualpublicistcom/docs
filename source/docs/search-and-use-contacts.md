## How the Search Bar Works

1. **Log in** to your account.  
2. In the sidebar, click **Search** to open the sleek search bar.  
3. **Type** a name, role, genre, outlet, or keyword — matching contacts appear instantly.

Each contact shows key info at a glance:

- **Company name**  
- **Location**  
- **Position**  
- **About**  
- **Genres**  
- **Tags**

Everything appears **in one place** — no extra tabs, no chaos, just results.

## Add Contacts in One Click

- Found someone interesting? Click the **three dots** to **Add to List** or **Add to Campaign** directly.  
- Short on time? Use **checkboxes** to **select multiple contacts** and add them all at once.  
- Changed your mind? Hit **Deselect All** and start fresh.

> Think of the search bar as your **backstage pass** to PR connections — organize, connect, and move faster than ever.
